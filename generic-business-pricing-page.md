# Frequently Asked Questions

## Which package should I choose?

Choose **Web Lite** when you only need a professional online presence and easy customer contact.

Choose **Web Pro** when customers need to book, register, reserve, request quotations, or submit inquiries, and you need a dashboard to manage those activities.

Choose **Cloud ERP** when you need to manage daily business operations such as customers, staff, sales, inventory, expenses, reports, projects, memberships, or multiple branches.

## Can the packages be customized for my industry?

Yes. Page names, customer workflows, dashboard sections, terminology, and ERP modules will be adjusted to match your business.

## Can I purchase a website and ERP together?

Yes. Web Lite or Web Pro can be combined with Cloud ERP to provide both a public website and a complete internal management system.

## Does Web Lite include a dashboard?

No. Web Lite is a professional informational website without an admin dashboard.

## Does Web Pro include a dashboard?

Yes. Web Pro includes a secure dashboard for managing website content, customer inquiries, bookings, services, staff, and relevant business details.

## Does Cloud ERP include a public website?

No. A public website is not included unless Web Lite or Web Pro is purchased separately.

## Are hosting and domain charges included?

Only when clearly stated in the final quotation. Otherwise, domain, hosting, email, and third-party service charges are separate.

## Are AI and WhatsApp services free?

The development and integration cost will be included in the quotation when selected. External AI, WhatsApp, SMS, email, and platform usage fees may be charged separately by the provider.

## Can new features be added later?

Yes. New features and modules can be added later and will be quoted separately.

## Do you provide support after launch?

Basic handover is included. Ongoing maintenance, content management, training, server management, and priority support can be purchased separately.
